## Overview

Hivemind Solutions was engaged by the Kiribati Institute of Technology (KIT) to support a critical stage in their digital journey.

Following the transition of network responsibilities from a previous service provider, Hivemind was tasked with ensuring knowledge transfer, assessing existing systems, and establishing a sustainable IT management model.

## Our Approach

During an in-country engagement, Hivemind conducted a comprehensive assessment of KIT’s IT infrastructure.

This included reviewing existing systems, identifying gaps, and laying the foundation for future upgrades, including improvements to KIT Betio and its remote teaching facilities.

A key focus of the engagement was capacity building. Hivemind delivered hands-on training sessions to the IT Support Team, ensuring they could independently manage and maintain the institute’s environment.

## Outcomes

* Completed a full assessment of KIT’s current IT systems
* Developed recommendations for infrastructure improvements and upgrades
* Strengthened internal IT capability through practical training and mentoring
* Supported long-term sustainability through a focus on local capacity and knowledge transfer

## Impact

This engagement represented more than a technical transition.

It established a more sustainable and self-reliant approach to IT operations, equipping the internal team with the capability and confidence to manage their environment moving forward.
