## Client Context

Vanuatu Skills Partnership operates with a small IT team working across multiple locations. The team manages cyber security, networks and day-to-day technology in an environment where urgent issues sometimes require a quick response.

## Challenge

The organisation operates with a small IT team spread across locations, responsible for maintaining cyber security, networks, and day-to-day systems.

This created a need for:

* consistent access to experienced technical guidance
* support across a broad range of technologies
* ongoing capability development to keep pace with change

At the same time, the internal team needed to remain hands-on and in control of their environment.

## Approach

Hivemind works alongside the internal team in a virtual advisory role. This model was chosen to give the team regular access to experienced guidance while preserving local ownership and capability.

## Delivery

The ongoing service includes:

* fortnightly technical reviews of current priorities and issues
* structured training based on the team's environment and responsibilities
* on-demand support when urgent issues need escalation
* practical advice that the internal team can apply and maintain

## Outcome

The partnership gives the internal team a consistent advisory rhythm and a responsive escalation path. Training and regular reviews support capability development while the team retains control of its systems and decisions.

For organisations with a small or distributed IT function, this model provides access to senior advice without replacing the people who understand the local environment.

## Technology and Capabilities Used

* virtual IT advisory support
* cyber security and network guidance
* technical reviews and escalation
* training and knowledge transfer
