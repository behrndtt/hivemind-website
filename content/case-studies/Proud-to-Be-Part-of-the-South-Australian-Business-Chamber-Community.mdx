# Overview

Hivemind Solutions is proud to be a member of the South Australian Business Chamber.

As an Adelaide-based Microsoft specialist supporting organisations across Australia, being part of the Chamber reflects our commitment to the South Australian business community and the organisations that help it grow.

This membership gives us the opportunity to connect with local business leaders, contribute to a broader professional network, and share our perspective on the role of secure, governed, and well-managed Microsoft environments in modern organisations.

# Why We Joined

For Hivemind, joining the South Australian Business Chamber is about connection, credibility, and community.

We value being part of a network that brings together businesses across industries, supports local capability, and creates opportunities for meaningful conversations between South Australian organisations.

As Microsoft specialists, we work with organisations that rely on Microsoft 365, Azure, Intune, Defender, Entra ID, Teams, Power Platform, Copilot readiness, licensing, and governance. The Chamber provides a valuable platform for us to connect with businesses that understand the importance of trusted advice, operational maturity, and strong technology foundations.

# What It Represents

Our membership reflects who we are as a business.

Hivemind is based in Adelaide, works Australia-wide, and remains strongly connected to the South Australian business community. We believe in practical advice, long-term relationships, and helping organisations make better decisions about the Microsoft platforms they already depend on.

Being part of the Chamber allows us to stand alongside other South Australian businesses and contribute to a network built on connection, capability, and shared growth.

# Looking Ahead

As Hivemind continues to grow, we remain focused on helping mature organisations secure, govern, support, and optimise their Microsoft environments.

Our membership with the South Australian Business Chamber is one way we stay connected to the local business community while continuing to support organisations across Australia.
