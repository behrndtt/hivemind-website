## The Challenge

The organisation operates with a small IT team spread across locations, responsible for maintaining cyber security, networks, and day-to-day systems.

This created a need for:

* consistent access to experienced technical guidance
* support across a broad range of technologies
* ongoing capability development to keep pace with change

At the same time, the internal team needed to remain hands-on and in control of their environment.

## Our Approach

Hivemind works alongside the internal IT team in a virtual advisory capacity.

**This includes:**

* fortnightly technical check-ins to review current priorities and challenges
* structured training sessions to build capability and keep skills up to date
* on-demand support for urgent issues as they arise

The focus is on practical guidance, clear communication, and ensuring the team can confidently manage their environment.

## Outcomes

* Improved stability across the organisation’s IT environment
* Stronger cyber security and operational oversight
* Increased capability within the internal IT team
* Ongoing progress through regular, structured engagement

## Impact

The engagement has enabled the organisation to maintain a well-functioning environment while continuing to build internal capability.

Access to responsive advisory support ensures challenges can be addressed quickly, while the internal team retains full ownership and control of their systems.
